Trust over Time

Green line shows trust (−1 to +1). Shaded band shows uncertainty: wide band = low certainty, narrow band = high certainty. Note how trust rises slowly with repeated positive interactions but can drop sharply after a single negative one.

What You Are Seeing

  • Asymmetric trust: Positive interactions nudge trust up; negative interactions pull it down faster.
  • Temporal memory: Each interaction updates an evolving history; recent events matter more.
  • Uncertainty: Certainty grows as entities interact. Early on, trust is volatile; later it stabilizes.
  • Web4 connection: This is a 1D slice of Web4's T3 trust tensor: a role-contextual trust score evolving over time.

In full Web4, trust is bound to Linked Context Tokens (LCTs) and roles, and propagates across an MRH graph. This visualizer focuses on one relationship to make the dynamics tangible.
